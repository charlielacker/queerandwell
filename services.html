Services

I strive to be as flexible as possible to try and meet your needs as best as I can. From on-going sessions with weekly support and frequent check-ins, to a six week challenge to get you started and able to continue independently - I've got you covered. Please contact me for a free consultation so we can set up the best plan for you!

  1. 1

    Free Consultation

    A short, one-on-one meeting to go over goals and availability.

  2. 2

    First Session

    We'll go over some forms, and start putting together a plan.

  3. 3

    Plan

    You'll receive your tailored plan, and we'll put things into motion!

  4. 4

    Progess

    Weekly meetings, and direct messaging capabilities to help you stay motivated and on track. Of course, your plan will be updated as you progress.

Pricing

I offer a sliding scale payment system based mainly on income, with some other considerations available. Below, you'll find a pricing example for weekly virtual meetings with a wellness plan for exercise and habit development. Pricing will differ depending on how you'd like to set up your coaching experience! Choosing a higher tier, when able, helps to support those in lower tiers. Following your free consultation, I'll provide you with more accurate pricing information.

  1. Tier 1

    $100

    Per Month

    This tier is appropriate for those who make less then $50,000 per year. Some other considerations for choosing this tier include being: a person who is under insured, a sex worker, an unpaid community organizer, someone supporting family members in need.

  2. Tier 2

    $150

    Per Month

    This tier is appropriate for those who make between $50,000 and $80,000 per year.

  3. Tier 3

    $250

    Per Month

    This tier is appropriate for those who make more than $80,000 per year. Some other considerations for choosing this tier include: owning the home you live in, having retirement funds or investments, working part time by choice, the ability to travel recreationally.
